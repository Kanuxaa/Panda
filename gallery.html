Pictures of the work done together by members of Team 08 from ADA University and students of No 310 school from the Hour of Code event









Team 08 participated in the Hour of Code on February 26, 2023, at Secondary School No. 310 in Baku, Azerbaijan. As team 08, we presented students with ADA University and the code.org program. After informing the students about how they can get admission to ADA University, we moved on to the fun part of our event. We introduced the students to the code.org program and solved several puzzles together. After the event, the majority of students expressed gratitude for the chance to write codes and expressed a greater interest in the IT industry. The project was successful in encouraging students with slides about ADA University and computer science while exposing them to coding and programming in an interesting way. At the end of our interesting event, we sincerely took pictures together.
